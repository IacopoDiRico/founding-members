How it works

Three steps.
One trusted community.

Deliberate by design. Every member verified. Every number permanent.

Step 01
Apply as a verified founder

Tell us what you're building, your stage and where you're based. No pitch deck required. A short form and a LinkedIn or Companies House link. Reviewed personally.

Step 02
Receive your founding number

If approved, you're issued a permanent serial number. #001 goes to the first external member. Numbers never change — they're your badge of when you believed.

Step 03
Access the full platform

Browse and offer perks. Attend founder and VC events. Connect with the community. When fundraising launches, raise from and invest in other verified founders.
