How it works

Three steps.
One trusted community.

We keep the process deliberate. Every member is reviewed. Every number is permanent.

Step 01
Apply as a verified founder

Tell us what you're building, your stage, and where you're based. No pitch deck. No fees. A short form and a LinkedIn or Companies House link. We review every application personally.

Step 02
Receive your founding number

If approved, you're issued a permanent serial number. #001 goes to the first external member. Numbers never change — they're your badge of when you believed. The lower the number, the earlier you were.

Step 03
Access the full platform

Browse and offer perks. Attend founder and VC events. Post in the community. When the fundraising layer launches, raise from and invest in other verified founders. The platform earns when you win — never from you.
